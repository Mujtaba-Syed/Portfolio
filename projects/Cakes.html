Cakes by Rimi is a delightful web application crafted with Django and Bootstrap, showcasing a wide array of artisanal cakes and desserts. Developed by Mujtaba Gillani, the platform offers a seamless browsing experience for customers to explore and order custom cakes tailored to their preferences. Leveraging Django's robust backend capabilities, the site ensures secure user authentication, order management, and real-time updates on order statuses. Bootstrap's responsive design elements enhance the user interface, providing a visually appealing and user-friendly experience across devices. "Cakes by Rimi" embodies creativity and functionality, catering to cake enthusiasts with a passion for quality and personalized confectionery.
